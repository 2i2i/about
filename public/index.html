chrony lounge

chrony : a Guest who is waiting in chronological order ~ the amount of its support is fixed by the Host, called min. first come, first serve

high roller lounge

high roller: a Guest who offers higher than the min support ~ higher support, first serve

eccentric lounge

eccentric: a Guest who offers support using coins of subjective value ~ e.g., an eccentric could offer its Host art or ticket NFT’s

lurkers lounge

lurker: a Guest who wants to indicate that the min is too high ~ lurkers only get served if its presence motivates the Host to lower its min, changing a lurker into a chrony or high roller

the philosophy behind 2i2i

2 fundamental elements of nature are info and energy ~ everyone in the world has both info and energy to offer ~ the energy of the Guest can come in the form of any coin* ~ the info of the Host comes in the form of a live video call ~ this makes 2i2i an efficient exchange between info and energy

the value of info is always greater than 0, whether miniscule or "major league" ~ in the old world, too low values could not be transmitted ~ now you could share a trillionth of a ButtCoin with a random person across the globe, just for hanging out

allowing the Guest to offer energy in any coin, of any value, makes 2i2i the most open market possible ~ hence the energy that u receive on 2i2i is the fairest energy in exchange for whatever u have to offer

*energy ~ coin : proof: expend energy (e.g. cut grass), receive coin (e.g. $), keep coin over time, give coin (e.g. $) to expend energy (e.g. get pool cleaned) => coin is a storage of energy, its leakage is called inflation ~ having access to coins gives one access to any other form of energy, with natural losses during transfer
